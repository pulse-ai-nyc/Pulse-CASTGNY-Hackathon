# Tip 9: Build Comparison & List Content

**What it is:** Pages on the brand's site (or third-party placements) that directly compare the brand to competitors, or include the brand in "best of" lists.

**How to measure it:**
- Search for "[brand] vs [competitor]" and "best [category] tools/programs" — does the brand appear?
- Check if the brand's own site has comparison or "alternatives" pages
- Check if the brand is included in third-party listicles and roundups

**Example:**
> Search: "Notion vs Linear"  
> Linear has a dedicated "Linear vs Notion" comparison page → models can cite it  
> Your brand has nothing → models skip you in comparison queries

**Why it matters:**
A huge volume of AI queries are comparative ("best X", "X vs Y", "alternatives to X"). If the brand has no presence in comparison content, it will be absent from these high-intent answers — which are often the ones closest to a purchase decision.

**What your agent should do:**
- Identify which comparison queries the brand is missing from
- Generate a comparison page outline (e.g. "Brand vs Competitor A: features, pricing, use cases")
- Recommend outreach to get included in third-party listicles
