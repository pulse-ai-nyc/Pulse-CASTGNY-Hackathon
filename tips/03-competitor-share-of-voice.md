# Tip 3: Compare Share of Voice vs Competitors

**What it is:** Your brand's mention count compared to each competitor across the same set of queries.

**How to measure it:**
- Across all queries and models, count total mentions per entity (brand + competitors)
- Calculate each entity's share: mentions / total mentions across all entities

**Example:**
> Across 5 queries × 2 models (10 responses):  
> Competitor A mentioned 8 times, Your brand 4 times, Competitor B 6 times  
> Your share of voice = 4/18 = **22%**

**Why it matters:**
SoM tells you if you appear. Share of voice tells you **how much space you occupy** relative to the competition. A brand might appear in every answer but always as an afterthought behind 3 competitors.

**What your agent should do:**
- Build a competitor comparison table (brand vs each competitor: mention count, %, avg position)
- Highlight the top-ranked competitor and explain why they dominate
