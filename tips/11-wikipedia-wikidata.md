# Tip 11: Establish Wikipedia & Wikidata Presence

**What it is:** Whether the brand has a Wikipedia article and/or a Wikidata entity — two of the most commonly referenced knowledge sources by AI models.

**How to measure it:**
- Search Wikipedia for the brand name — does an article exist?
- Search Wikidata for the brand — is there a structured entity?
- Compare to competitors: do they have Wikipedia pages?

**Example:**
> "Semester at Sea" has a Wikipedia article with history, curriculum, and citations  
> "Global Edge" has no Wikipedia page  
> → AI models default to recommending Semester at Sea because it has a trusted, structured source

**Why it matters:**
Wikipedia is one of the most-cited sources in LLM training data and retrieval pipelines. A brand with a Wikipedia presence is dramatically more likely to appear in AI answers. Wikidata adds structured facts (founded, headquarters, type) that models can pull directly.

**What your agent should do:**
- Check if the brand has a Wikipedia article and Wikidata entity
- If missing, generate a draft Wikipedia-style summary (with proper citations) as a starting point
- Flag this as a high-impact action item
