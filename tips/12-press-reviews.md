# Tip 12: Get Press Coverage & Reviews

**What it is:** Recent mentions of the brand in news articles, review platforms (G2, Capterra, Trustpilot), and industry publications.

**How to measure it:**
- Count recent press mentions (last 6–12 months)
- Check presence on major review sites relevant to the category
- Check if any press/review URLs are cited in AI model responses

**Example:**
> Competitor appears on G2 with 500+ reviews and was featured in TechCrunch last month  
> Your brand has no review-site presence and no recent press  
> → Models have no recent, third-party validation to cite for your brand

**Why it matters:**
AI models heavily weight third-party validation. Press and reviews serve as independent confirmation that the brand is real, active, and worth recommending. Without them, the model has no reason to prefer your brand over a competitor with rich external coverage.

**What your agent should do:**
- Search for recent press mentions and review-site profiles
- Compare coverage volume and recency to competitors
- Recommend PR outreach or review-site registration as actionable next steps
