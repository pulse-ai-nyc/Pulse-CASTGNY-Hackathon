# Tip 15: Align Content With Query Language

**What it is:** Whether the brand's page titles, headings, and intro copy use the same language and phrases that users type into AI models.

**How to measure it:**
- List your high-intent test queries (e.g. "best international business programs")
- Check if the brand's pages contain those exact phrases in H1s, meta titles, or opening paragraphs
- Compare to competitors: do their pages match query language more closely?

**Example:**
> Query: "best CRM for startups"  
> Competitor's H1: "The Best CRM for Startups" → direct match  
> Your brand's H1: "Our Platform" → no match  
> → The model picks the competitor because the content explicitly answers the query

**Why it matters:**
AI models (especially those using retrieval) match user queries against page content. If your headings and copy don't contain the words users are searching for, your pages won't be retrieved — even if your product is a perfect fit.

**What your agent should do:**
- Compare the brand's key page headings/titles against the test query set
- Flag mismatches ("Your H1 says 'Our Platform' but users search for 'best CRM for startups'")
- Recommend specific title and heading rewrites
